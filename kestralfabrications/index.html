A design studio for industrial work, in the making.

Kestral Fabrications is in an exploratory phase: a design-led studio building toward custom industrial work, with parts produced through a mix of in-house build and a vetted network of fabrication partners. Initial focus is architectural fixtures, retail interiors, and small-batch industrial work in steel, aluminum, copper, rotomold, and fiberglass.

03 / Process

How the work will run.

01

Concept & design

From brief through CAD, DFM, and material selection. Output is a fabrication-ready package — STEP, DXF, or shop prints — sized for either in-house build or vendor production.

02

Prototype

Single-piece proofs for fit, finish, and tolerance before any run commits. Routed to whichever path — in-house or partner shop — best fits the material and method. Target turnaround: ten working days.

03

Production routing

Each job is routed by material, volume, and finish: simple sheet and weldments built in-shop; rotomold, fiberglass, anodizing, and higher-volume runs sent to qualified partners. Vendor selection and oversight handled by us.

04

QA & delivery

Dimensional check against print, finish QA, and packaging photographed before shipment — same standard whether built in-shop or by a partner. Local delivery in-metro; freight elsewhere.
