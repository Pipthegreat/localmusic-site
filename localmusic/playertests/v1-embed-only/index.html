Player test · V1

Embed-Only

A single Spotify iframe, controlled by the iframe-API. Row taps call loadUri() then a backoff retry on play(). Mobile keeps the iframe visible — visibility is non-negotiable for iOS audio autoplay.

Thesis: Spotify owns playback. We own data. The page never tries to be cleverer than the embed. Trade: If the visitor is already playing Spotify elsewhere, Connect silently rejects play() — and we surface a stall banner. No fallback.
