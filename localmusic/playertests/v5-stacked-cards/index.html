Player test · V5

Stacked Cards

Every track gets its own Spotify embed. Fifteen independent iframes, no IFrame-API controller, no loadUri() juggling, no stall detection. Each card has its own play button — the visitor manages their own audio.

Thesis: Spotify's own embed is good. Don't wrap it. Don't coordinate across instances. Stop trying to be a player and become a list of players. Trade: 15 iframes is real weight (~6MB+ first paint). Mitigated by loading="lazy" — embeds below the fold don't initialize until scrolled. No skip-next, no auto-advance.
