Embed + an un-throttled watchdog

Keep the embed, but stop trusting its events. A Web Worker (whose timers browsers don't throttle) ticks once a second, a silent AudioContext anchor keeps the tab media-active, and we extrapolate the song position from wall-clock time — so when the iframe goes quiet in the background, we can still tell when the track ends and load the next one.
