Push the queue to Spotify's servers

The turning point. When you press play, we start the track and POST the next fifteen songs to Spotify's own /me/player/queue. Advancing track-to-track becomes Spotify's job, not a client timer's — the strongest defense a browser tab has against the background-stop bug. Honest caveat: the tab is still a suspendable device, so at a song boundary a hard-suspended tab can stall; for a guaranteed background, hand off to a native device (see v8).
