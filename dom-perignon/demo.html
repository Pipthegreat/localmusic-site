A design studio for industrial work, in the making.

Kestrel Fabrications is in an exploratory phase: a design-led studio building toward custom industrial work, with parts produced through a mix of in-house build and a vetted network of fabrication partners.

01 / Capabilities

What the studio will do.

Design

From brief to drawings

Concept, CAD, DFM review, and material selection - delivered as a fabrication-ready package.

In-house build

Where it fits the work

Sheet metal, weldments, and small assemblies built in-shop when material, volume, and method line up.

Partner network

Outsourced production

Rotomold, fiberglass, anodizing, and higher-volume runs routed through vetted partners.

Project management

Prototype to delivery

Prototype, finish QA, and delivery managed end-to-end - same standard whether in-shop or partner-built.
